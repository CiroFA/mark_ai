However, any future debt may contain limitations on our ability to (i) pay cash upon conversion or redemption of the Outstanding Convertible Notes, which may require us to elect to deliver solely shares of our class A common stock to settle such conversion (other than paying cash in lieu of delivering any fractional share), or (ii) sell certain bitcoin to generate cash that can be used to make such cash payments.

The Company has an
insider trading policy
("Insider Trading Policy") that governs the purchase, sale and other dispositions of Walmart securities by its directors, officers, associates and the Company itself. The Insider Trading Policy states, among other things, that our directors, officers and associates are prohibited from trading in such securities while in possession of material, nonpublic information. The Company is also prohibited from trading in Walmart securities while in possession of material, nonpublic information related to the Company unless such trading activity complies with all applicable securities laws. The Company believes the Insider Trading Policy is reasonably designed to promote compliance with insider trading laws, rules and regulations, and any applicable NYSE listing standards. The foregoing summary of our Insider Trading Policy does not purport to be complete and is qualified by reference to the Insider Trading Policy filed as Exhibit 19 to this Annual Report on Form 10-K.
